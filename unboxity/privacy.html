Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Fiftify Inc. ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Unboxity mobile application ("App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data transparently.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: email address, display name, and profile picture (when using Google Sign-In)
  • Box and item data: photos of box contents, item names, descriptions, quantities, dimensions, and values you enter
  • Location labels: names and descriptions of storage locations you create (e.g., "Kitchen", "Garage")

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information: device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage data: app interactions, feature usage, crash reports, and performance data (via Sentry)
  • Network information: connectivity status (online/offline) for sync functionality
  • Advertising identifiers: used by Google AdMob to serve relevant ads in the free version

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google Sign-In: basic profile information (name, email, profile photo) when you choose to sign in with Google
  • RevenueCat: purchase and subscription status information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the service: store your box inventory, sync data across sessions, generate QR codes
  • AI item recognition: photos you submit are sent to Google Gemini (via OpenRouter) for item identification. Photos are processed in real-time and are not stored by the AI provider beyond the processing request
  • Manage subscriptions: process purchases and verify premium status via RevenueCat
  • Display advertisements: show relevant ads via Google AdMob in the free version
  • Improve the App: analyze crash reports and usage patterns via Sentry to fix bugs and improve performance
  • Send notifications: push notifications for sync status, updates, or important account information (with your permission)

4. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data with the following third-party services only as necessary to operate the App:

Supabase

Purpose: Backend database, user authentication, file storage, and real-time sync. Data stored: account information, box/item data, photos.
Supabase Privacy Policy

Google Sign-In

Purpose: Optional authentication method. Data shared: OAuth tokens. Data received: name, email, profile picture.
Google Privacy Policy

OpenRouter / Google Gemini

Purpose: AI-powered item recognition from photos. Data shared: photos of box contents (processed in real-time, not retained).
OpenRouter Privacy Policy

RevenueCat

Purpose: Subscription and in-app purchase management. Data shared: anonymous user ID, purchase history.
RevenueCat Privacy Policy

Google AdMob

Purpose: Displaying advertisements in the free version. Data shared: advertising identifiers, device info, general location.

Sentry

Purpose: Error tracking, crash reporting, and performance monitoring. Data shared: device info, OS version, stack traces, breadcrumbs (no personal content).
Sentry Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase-hosted infrastructure and locally on your device using encrypted local storage (AsyncStorage). Data is transmitted using TLS encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

6. Offline Data & Synchronization

The App stores data locally on your device to enable offline functionality. This includes box data, item lists, and cached photos. When an internet connection is restored, local changes are automatically synchronized with our servers. Locally stored data persists until you clear the app data or uninstall the App.

7. Camera & Photo Library Access

The App requests access to your device camera and photo library. Camera access is used to photograph box contents and scan QR codes. Photo library access allows you to select existing photos for item recognition. Photos are uploaded to our servers for storage and sent to the AI service for recognition. You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device settings.

8. Push Notifications

With your permission, we may send push notifications for sync status updates, app updates, or important account information. You can disable push notifications at any time through your device settings.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is rated for ages 3+ but account creation requires users to be at least 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: request a portable copy of your data
  • Opt-out: disable personalized ads through your device advertising settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below or use the account management features in the App settings.

11. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data from our servers within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics purposes. Crash reports and performance data are retained by Sentry for 90 days.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or via email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when this policy was last revised.
