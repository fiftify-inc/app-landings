Account deletion request process

How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your Fiftify account and all associated data, please send an email to:

support@fiftify.com

Include the following in your request:

  • The email address associated with your Fiftify account
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

What Gets Deleted

Upon processing your request, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account credentials and profile information
  • Push notification tokens and device associations
  • Any cached business data on our servers related to your mobile app usage

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receiving your email. You will receive a confirmation once your account has been deleted.

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed
  • You will lose access to the Fiftify mobile app immediately after deletion
  • We may retain certain data as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., transaction records for tax compliance), as described in our Privacy Policy
